##  About Harvestly Games

Harvestly Games is a farming simulation experience inspired by classic social farming games, reimagined for modern online communities.

Players can:

- 🌾 Grow and harvest crops
- 🐄 Raise livestock
- 🏭 Craft valuable goods
- 🛒 Trade resources with other players
- 🏡 Expand and customize farms
- 🎯 Complete quests and challenges
- 🤝 Participate in community events
- 💰 Earn rewards through gameplay

Whether you're a casual farmer or a dedicated tycoon, Harvestly Games offers a rewarding agricultural adventure.

### 🌾 Farming System

- Plant seasonal crops

- Unlock rare seeds

### 🐄 Livestock Management

- Raise farm animals
- Produce milk, eggs, wool, and more
- Upgrade barns and facilities
- Breed premium livestock

### 🏭 Crafting & Production

- Process harvested materials
- Create artisan products
- Unlock advanced recipes
- Build production chains

### 🛒 Trading Economy

### 🏡 Farm Expansion
